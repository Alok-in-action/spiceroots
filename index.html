The Nawab Legacy

From Roots to Plate

In the heart of Madhya Pradesh lies a small town called Berchha — a town that nurtured Nawab Brothers, from a thriving kirana shop to a century-old and respected name in traditional groceries. The firm built its reputation on purity, trust, and deep knowledge of spices.

From this legacy emerged Nawab Overseas — a sister concern founded in 2025 with a global vision but rooted in generational wisdom.

For over 100 years, the art of identifying superior-quality raw spices — judging them by aroma, texture, oil content, and origin — has been carefully passed down within the family. Today, that same inherited expertise guides our procurement process. We do not merely buy spices; we select them with discernment shaped by decades of experience.

SpiceRoots – Royal Heritage Reimagined

Under Nawab Overseas, SpiceRoots was created to carry this legacy beyond borders.

I Purity Without Compromise

All SpiceRoots masalas are:

  • ✦ Free from preservatives
  • ✦ Free from artificial food colors
  • ✦ Free from harmful essences or synthetic chemicals

"We believe flavor should come from nature — not laboratories."

II Health-Conscious Formulation

Unlike many commercial blends, our masalas contain minimal or no common salt. This allows:

  • ✦ Better control over sodium intake
  • ✦ Greater flexibility for home cooks and chefs
  • ✦ Healthier everyday consumption

"Our products are crafted for families who value both taste and wellbeing."

III Authentic Aroma & Natural Strength

Even our "common" masalas are elevated in quality. Because we prioritize:

  • ✦ High volatile oil content in spices
  • ✦ Natural ingredients over fillers
  • ✦ Fresh grinding and careful blending

"The result is deeper aroma, richer flavor release, and a more authentic culinary experience."

Every Blend Tells a Story

Each pack of SpiceRoots carries the memory of royal kitchens and regional traditions, for instance:

  • Rajshahi Biryani Masala echoes the grandeur of regal feasts.
  • Malwi Fish Fry Masala reflects the earthy vibrance of Central India.
  • Kadhi Gosht Masala carries the warmth of nawabi hospitality.
  • Shahi Garam Masala embodies the depth and dignity of royal Indian kitchens, where every spice speaks in harmony.
  • Kitchen King Masala celebrates everyday mastery — the crown jewel of versatile home-style cooking.
  • Pindi Chhole Masala captures the bold spirit and robust flavors of Punjab's traditional dhabas.
  • Mumbai Pav Bhaji Masala recreates the vibrant energy and irresistible zest of India's bustling street-food capital.

These are not just spice mixes — they are cultural expressions.
